How to Play Yaniv

Quick guide for your first game.

Goal: keep your score low.
Your turn: discard, then draw.
Say Yaniv: only at 5 points or less.

1. What You Do Each Turn

  1. Discard cards. You can play one card, a set, or a run.
  2. Draw one card. Take from the deck or from the discard pile choices.
  3. Check your total. If your hand is 5 or less, you can call Yaniv.

3. Calling Yaniv

You can call Yaniv only when your hand is 5 or less. If someone else has the same or lower total, you get +30 points (Assaf).

4. Score Rules

  • After each round, add points to your score.
  • If you land exactly on 50 or 100, subtract 50.
  • If your score goes over 100, you are out.

5. Optional Move: Slamdown

Some games use Slamdown. If your game has it, here is the simple version:

  • Play your discard, then draw from the deck.
  • If the drawn card continues your discard, you can slam it down right away.
  • You cannot slamdown your last card.
  • Slamdown is only for human-only games.

Example: you play 5♥ 6♥, draw 7♥, then slam down 7♥.
