Yaniv

The fast-paced card game

How to Play

  • Each player starts with 5 cards. On your turn: discard card(s), then draw one.
  • You can discard a single card, a set (2+ same rank), or a run (3+ consecutive same suit).
  • Jokers are wild-cards that can be played in a set, and are worth 0 points
  • When your hand totals 5 or less points, you can call Yaniv!
  • If someone else has same or fewer points â€” you're Assaf'ed (+30 pts).
  • Score resets -50 if you hit exactly 50 or 100 points after a round. Players exceeding 100 are eliminated.
  • Card values: Joker = 0 | Ace = 1  |  2-9 = face value  |  10/J/Q/K = 10
  • Last player wins!
